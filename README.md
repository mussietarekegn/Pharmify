## 📖 About The Project

**Pharmify** is more than just a pharmacy management system—it is a comprehensive, multi-vendor marketplace connecting patients directly with local pharmacies. It acts as both a marketplace to find medications and an intelligent health assistant.

Built with a robust **Django backend** and a blazing-fast **Next.js frontend**, Pharmify ensures a seamless experience for three distinct user roles: Customers, Pharmacy Owners, and System Admins.

---

## ✨ Core Features (Role-Based Access)

### 🧑‍t Customer Portal
* **🔍 Smart Search & Filtering:** Find medications instantly by price, rating, location, and category.
* **🤖 AI Symptom Checker:** Get intelligent, AI-driven guidance on symptoms before making a purchase.
* **🔔 Real-Time Notifications:** Stay updated on order statuses, prescription approvals, and delivery tracking.
* **🛒 Secure Checkout:** A seamless cart experience for over-the-counter and prescription medications.

### 🏪 Pharmacy Owner Portal
* **🔐 Secure Registration:** Easy onboarding with document upload for fast verification.
* **📦 Product Management:** Add, edit, and manage medication listings (including images, pricing, and specific location availability).
* **📊 Inventory Tracking:** Keep track of stock levels and fulfill incoming customer orders efficiently.

### 🛡️ Admin Dashboard
* **✅ Vendor Moderation:** Review uploaded documents to approve or reject new Pharmacy Owners.
* **📋 Content Moderation:** Monitor listings, manage disputes, and oversee platform health.
